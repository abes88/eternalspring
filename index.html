Welcome to Eternal Spring Acupuncture

We are a full scale clinic of Traditional Chinese Medicine, offering acupuncture and a complete herbal pharmacy. Combining the ancient art of Chinese medicine with the knowledge of modern medical science, our treatment plans are tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual.

All services are provided by Minnesota State Licensed and National Certification Commission of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (NCCAOM) certified acupuncturists.
